ACHIEVEHOCKEY

Achieve is an international ice hockey recruitment agency dedicated to connecting players, parents, coaches, and academies with opportunities across the hockey world.

Our Story

At Achieve Hockey, we believe that every player has untapped potential waiting to be discovered. Our mission is to provide world-class training that develops not just hockey skills, but character, discipline, and a winning mindset.

We combine cutting-edge techniques with proven coaching methodologies to help players at all levels reach their highest potential.

Excellence

Our Journey

Key milestones in the growth of Achieve Hockey.

2010

Founded by former NHL player Mike Johnson with a vision to revolutionize hockey training.

2013

Expanded to include specialized goalie training programs and opened our second facility.

2016

Launched our first summer intensive program, attracting players from across the country.

2020

Adapted our programs for virtual training during the pandemic, reaching international players.

2023

Celebrated our 50th player receiving a college hockey scholarship through our programs.
