ACHIEVE

International Ice Hockey Recruitment Agency

Achieve is an international ice hockey recruitment agency dedicated to connecting players, parents, coaches, and academies with opportunities across the hockey world.

OUR STORY

Around 2017, a father of two student-athletes had a problem.

Like many other families today, pursuing Ice Hockey through Highschool and beyond was a mystery. He knew that his sons had to go to North America for a bigger stage, but he didn’t know where to start. After a journey full of uncertainty and discovery, he founded a company to help the next generation of student-athlete families like his.

This is the story of our Founder, Thomas Kim.
