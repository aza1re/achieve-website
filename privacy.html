Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 14, 2025

Achieve Hockey Edu ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information
We may collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo or avatar
  • Team or player information
  • Contact details (for team representatives or coaches)

b. Usage Data
We may automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Log data (such as access times and pages viewed)

c. Firebase Data
We use Firebase for authentication, database, and storage. Firebase may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Firebase’s Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App
  • Manage user accounts and authentication
  • Display tournaments, teams, camps, and player information
  • Communicate with users (e.g., notifications, updates)
  • Improve and personalize the App experience
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers (such as Firebase) to operate the App
  • If required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • With your consent or at your direction (e.g., sharing team rosters)

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for users involved in youth hockey and education. If you are under 13, please use the App with parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental permission.

6. Your Choices

  • Access or update your account information in the App
  • Request deletion of your account by contacting us

7. Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: achieve@achievehockey.org
