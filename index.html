TYPICAL WEEK AT THE CAMP

How does a typical week at the camp look like? A typical day on Camp includes two on-ice sessions alongside with two off-ice sessions. Third on-ice session is every evening's open ice, an optional hour, where everyone meets on the same ice to practice whatever they feel like, or develop individual skills in a private lesson. At the end of the week players get to show what they have learned in a final game. All off-ice trainings and games are supervised.

We know, that reconditioning after a whole day of trainings is also important, that's why we offer many opportunities to relax, like swimming pool, sauna, jacuzzi or massages.

Mid-week trip to Prague - On Wednesdays we organize a half-day trip to Prague. We want to show the participants all the beauties of the historical city center, but it's also a great way to take a break from all the trainings and relax a bit.

ON-ICE TRAININGS

Practice and learn an intensive type of Hockey game, under the professional supervision of experienced coaches. We allow the maximum number of 30 players on ice so the training sessions are as intensive and non-stop as you can imagine and manage.

It is not going to be that easy but you will feel the results through self-improvement, and self-satisfaction almost immediately. We believe this to be the best personal reward we can offer.

Our trainings are based on typical elements of Czech hockey trainings, like „Smart Game", Timing, crossing, breaking to open space, breakout, regrouping, neutral zone transition, rotation in corners, fore-checking, back-checking.

Training videos

OFF-ICE TRAINING

Not only do we have professionals on ice, but we make sure off-ice is just as focused and structured.

These trainings are a mixture of drills like jogging, hand-eye coordination games, stretching, agility development, stamina, quickness, flexibility, coordination or acrobatics, but there is also time for games, we like basketball, floorball, street hockey, Inline hockey, soccer, baseball, frisbee, mini-golf, and a variety of other recreational games. And of course a bit of fitness - circuit training, station training, plyometric, plus modern muscle development techniques.

Important part of each Camp is also Classroom, where coaches discuss player's techniques and skills during video review, as well as ice hockey theory, rules and tactics. And last but not least - we are trying to teach players personal responsibility, self-discipline, motivation and how to be a good team player.

PARENTS AND FAMILIES

MAKE THIS A FAMILY EVENT!

We welcome and take care of family members just as we do of our players. You can watch your children during hockey trainings, travel around Republic's most beautiful historical cities, while your kids will be under our supervision, plan bike trips, enjoy full access to sport facilities, or simply relax. And don't forget to pack your skates, so you can join them during evening's open ice sessions!

The price for a week stay is 490 EUR in a single room or 690 EUR in a double room in our Camp hotel.

What is included in the price? Accommodation and breakfast, Prague sightseeing trip, Bbq for parents and attending players and goalies, joining kids during open ice sessions.

Full board is optional for 180 EUR/week. More about packages

ACCOMODATION

Choose from two distinct hotel options - Camp Hotel or Parents Hotel.

PLAYER'S CAMP HOTEL: YOUR STAY AT CIHC

Located right by the ice arena, the Camp Hotel is part of your CIHC package, ideal for youth players. Accommodations are mainly 4 to 6-bed rooms, with a few 2 and 3-bed options (note: no single rooms). Each room includes an en suite bathroom and towels for convenience. Plus, our youth players benefit from 24-hour supervision, ensuring a safe and comfortable stay.

HOTEL BÍLÝ PÁV (PARENTS HOTEL): COMFORT AND PRIVACY NEARBY

Bílý Páv, our choice for parents and adult players, offers private, comfortable lodging just an 8-minute walk from the ice arena. Available for an additional fee, the hotel provides 2-bedroom en suites and single rooms, plus limited "Apartment rooms" with extra sofa beds. Each room features an en suite bathroom and towels. Enjoy the independence of this unsupervised option, complete with an in-house restaurant and parking.

TRANSPORTATION

Travel in comfort and style to the Camp site with our transfer options. Take the Hockey Shuttle, leaving from the Prague airport at noon on Camp day one, with a stop near the main train station in the city center. Or, elevate your journey with the Hockey taxi - a private car that can accommodate up to 9 passengers, anywhere and anytime.

To secure your transfer, book in advance through our email at Achieve@achievehockey.org. You'll receive a confirmation with all the details, ensuring a seamless arrival at the camp.

COACHING STAFF

Our experienced coaches are passionate about helping hockey players of all ages and skill levels achieve their goals. Our team of highly skilled professionals has played at the highest levels of hockey and is committed to providing personalised attention to each camper to develop their skills.

We have a diverse team of coaches from the Czechia, bringing different perspectives and techniques to offer a comprehensive training program. Our focus is not just on-ice training but also off-ice training, including conditioning, nutrition, and mental preparation. We aim to create a fun, safe, and supportive environment where our campers can thrive and develop a love for the sport of hockey.

At Czech International Hockey Camp, our goal is to help campers reach their full potential as athletes and individuals.

SUPERVISION

We understand that sending your child to camp can be a big decision. That's why at the Czech International Hockey Camp we want to assure parents that their children are in good hands with our team of supervisors.

Our supervisors are highly trained professionals with years of experience working with children. They are passionate about providing a safe and nurturing environment where children can learn and grow. They undergo extensive background checks and training to ensure they have the skills necessary to keep your child safe.

Our supervisors are always available to answer any questions or concerns parents may have during their child's time at camp. They work closely with our coaching staff to ensure each camper receives the attention needed to thrive.

We take the safety and well-being of our campers seriously, and our supervisors are an integral part of ensuring that our campers have a positive and memorable experience. Parents can feel confident leaving their children at the Czech International Hockey Camp, knowing they are in good hands with our team of experienced and dedicated supervisors.

ABOUT US

Achieve Hockey is dedicated to developing the next generation of hockey players through world-class training programs and personalized coaching. Our organization brings together elite coaches, state-of-the-art facilities, and proven training methodologies to help players reach their full potential on and off the ice.

Founded by passionate hockey professionals, we have created a unique training environment that combines technical skill development, tactical awareness, physical conditioning, and mental preparation. Our programs are designed for players of all ages and skill levels who are committed to excellence.

We believe in building not just better hockey players, but better people. Through our comprehensive approach to player development, we emphasize character, teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship. Our mission is to provide every athlete with the tools, knowledge, and support they need to achieve their hockey dreams.
